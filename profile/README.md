## 👋 你好
Open dump 是一个致力于恢复 UUP dump、TechBench dump 等项目透明度的开源组织。

我们认为，真正的安全不应以牺牲透明度为代价。  
以 “安全” 之名闭源，往往只是制造了不可审查的黑盒。  
这使得用户无法审查源代码，剥夺了用户验证安全性的权利。

我们坚信，只有保持开放、协作、共享，才能延续一个项目的生命力。  
因此，我们收集了这些项目已知的最后开源代码，对其进行必要的修复与更新后，继续在此开源。

## 👋 Hello
Open dump is an open-source organization dedicated to restoring transparency to the UUP dump and TechBench dump project.

We believe that true security should never come at the expense of transparency.  
Closing source code under the guise of "security" often results in nothing more than an un-auditable "black box."  
This practice prevents users from inspecting the source code and strips them of their right to verify its safety.

We firmly believe that openness, collaboration, and sharing are the only ways to sustain the vitality of a project.  
Therefore, we have gathered the last known open-source codes, applied necessary fixes and updates, and are continuing its development here as a fully open-source project.
